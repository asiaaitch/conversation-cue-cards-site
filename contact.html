At C3 For Me, our mission is to empower students with disabilities to succeed in life after high school. Our Conversation Cue Cards have been designed specifically for K12 districts and schools that serve these students, helping educators and administrators facilitate productive discussions that prepare students for the future.

But we know that parents play a crucial role in their children's success as well. That's why we're excited to offer our Conversation Cue Cards to parents, giving them the tools they need to support their children's transition to adulthood.

Whether you're a school district administrator, an educator, or a parent, we would love to connect with you and discuss how our Conversation Cue Cards can help you empower students with disabilities to reach their full potential.
